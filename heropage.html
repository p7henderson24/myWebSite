About Princess Diana

Diana Spencer was born on July 1st, 1961, and passed on August 31st, 1997 in a car accident. She has two children, William and harry, who now have kids of their own. During her lifetime, Diana was an activist who earned international stardom after marrying into the Royal family. She was born into British nobility and worked as a primary school teacher's assistant before she married Prince Charles.

Why She's My Hero

Diana is my hero because throughout her life she stood up for what she believed in, including her work with HIV/AIDS patients in the 1980's and 1990's despite controversy, her dedication to the HALO Trust, and her work with cancer reaserch in London and across the world. Throughout her life, Diania was a ray of light that brightened everyone that she came into contact with.
